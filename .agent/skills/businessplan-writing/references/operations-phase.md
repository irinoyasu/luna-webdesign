**You MUST synthesize information from ALL previously completed phases before drafting.**

If files already exist from a previous execution, you must incorporate their content to ensure continuity and avoid duplication.

## Detailed Sub-Task Instructions

### Operations Plan Template

**Dependencies:**
- **Phase 3: Solution:** Feature complexity and service model determine the Customer Success/Service requirements.
- **Phase 4: Technology:** Technical architecture dictates the required IT, DevOps, and Technology operations.
- **Phase 9: GTM:** Marketing and Sales channel strategies define the requirements for Marketing/Sales Ops.
- **Phase 6: Traction & Progress:** Operational readiness must be phased according to the release timing of product.

For each operational function (sub-tasks 2–16), document:

1. **Function Overview:** Purpose, scope, key responsibilities
2. **Key Processes:** Core workflows and procedures (include process diagrams where helpful)
3. **Team & Roles:** Required headcount, skills, reporting structure
4. **Tools & Systems:** Software, hardware, vendors
5. **SLAs & Metrics:** Service level agreements, performance KPIs
6. **Costs:** Budget estimate (people, tools, facilities)
7. **Risks & Dependencies:** What could go wrong, dependencies on other functions
8. **Phase 1 vs. Scale:** What to build for launch vs. what to add at scale

### Function-Specific Guidance

**Write Corporate Planning Operations:** Strategic planning cadence (weekly/monthly/quarterly), OKR/KPI framework, board meeting management, corporate governance, and long-term goal setting.

**Write Product Operations:** Managing the product feedback loop, roadmap prioritization mechanics, product usage analytics, and bridging the gap between Customer Success and Engineering.

**Write Technology Operations:** Development methodology (Agile/Scrum), sprint cadence, code review processes, testing (QA/automated), release management, and tech debt tracking.

**Write IT Operations:** Cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP/Azure), security policies (ISO27001/SOC2), developer toolchain, internal IT support, disaster recovery, and vendor management.

**Write Marketing Operations:** Marketing tech stack (MarTech), lead attribution models, campaign execution workflows, conversion tracking, and budget reporting.

**Write Sales Operations:** CRM management (Salesforce/HubSpot), sales funnel reporting, commission structures, sales enablement materials, and deal desk support.

**Write Customer Service Operations:** Support channels, ticketing systems (Zendesk), CSAT/NPS tracking, customer onboarding playbooks, and expansion/retention operations.

**Write Partnerships Operations:** Managing ecosystem partners, referral programs, strategic alliances, and onboarding/success paths for non-customer partners.

**Write Production Operations:** Core service delivery workflows, capacity planning, quality control, and continuous improvement loops.

**Write Logistics Operations:** (If applicable) Sourcing, inventory management, fulfillment, shipping logs, and return merchandise authorization (RMA) processes.

**Write Finance & Accounting Operations:** Chart of accounts, tax compliance, bookkeeping cadence, accounts payable/receivable (AP/AR), and financial reporting.

**Write Legal & Compliance Operations:** Intellectual Property (IP) filing strategy, contract lifecycle management, data privacy (GDPR/CCPA), company-wide compliance training, and regulatory affairs.

**Write HR & Talent Operations:** Recruitment pipeline (ATS), onboarding/offboarding, payroll, benefits administration, performance reviews, and company culture initiatives.

**Write Data & Analytics Operations:** Centralized data warehouse (BigQuery/Snowflake), BI dashboard management (Looker/Tableau), and data-driven decision-making support across all departments.

**Write IR & PR Operations:** Managing investor updates, PR strategy, media relations, crisis communications, and maintaining the corporate public voice.
