# Team Phase Reference

## Purpose

Define the organizational structure and human capital strategy. This phase builds the core team profile and recruitment plan to execute the business strategy.

## Inputs

**Process Dependencies:**
- **Phase 10: Operations (Mandatory):** Functional plans and departmental requirements determine the necessary roles and headcount for the organization chart.

**You MUST synthesize information from ALL previously completed phases before drafting.** 
The business plan is a cumulative pipeline. Your inputs include:
1. **Previous Phase Outputs:** All documents and research logs generated in Phases 1-10.
2. **Existing Workspace Data:** Always check the output directory (`{output_folder}/businessplan-writing/`) for any existing `team/` folder or data from preceding phases. If files already exist from a previous execution, you must incorporate their content to ensure continuity and avoid duplication.
3. **Primary Direct Dependencies:** Phase 10: Operations (functional plans and reporting structures).

## Detailed Sub-Task Instructions

### 1. Create Organization Chart

**Dependencies:**
- **Phase 10: Operations:** Use the functional lanes (Corporate, Product, Tech, Sales, etc.) and departmental needs to structure the org chart.
- **Phase 6: Traction:** Use the growth roadmap to define when specific roles need to be filled based on the release timing of product.

Design the organizational structure:
- Current org chart (if team exists)
- Target org chart at key milestones (launch, Series A, 50 employees, 100 employees)
- Reporting relationships and span of control
- Functional vs. cross-functional team design
- Advisory board and board of directors structure

### 2. Write Company Profile

**Dependencies:**
- **Phase 1: Research:** Use the historical company background and founding story.
- **Phase 12: Purpose:** Use the mission and vision for the qualitative parts of the profile.
- Legal name and entity type (LLC, C-Corp, K.K., etc.)
- Date of incorporation and jurisdiction
- Registered address and operational locations
- Company registration numbers
- Capital structure (authorized shares, outstanding shares)
- Brief history and founding story
- Key milestones achieved

### 3. Write Corporate Culture

**Dependencies:**
- **Phase 12: Purpose:** Values and norms must reflect the core purpose and long-term vision.

Define organizational culture:
- Core values (3–5 values with behavioral examples)
- Culture principles (how the team works together)
- Communication norms (meetings, async, tools)
- Decision-making framework
- Diversity, equity, and inclusion commitments
- Work style (remote, hybrid, in-office)
- Traditions and rituals

### 4. Write Recruitment Policy

**Dependencies:**
- **Phase 13: Financials:** Compensation strategy and hiring cadence must align with budget and runway constraints.
- **Phase 9: GTM:** Sales/Marketing quotas and capacity needs drive specific recruitment volumes.

Plan talent acquisition:
- Hiring priorities by phase (roles needed at each stage)
- Job descriptions for critical early hires
- Compensation philosophy (salary ranges, equity, benefits)
- Sourcing strategy (channels, referrals, agencies)
- Interview process and evaluation criteria
- Onboarding plan
- Employer branding

### 5. Write Team Member Profiles
4: 
**Dependencies:**
- **Phase 1: Research:** Use the initial founder pedigree and advisor data captured in the research phase.
- **Sub-task 1 (Org Chart):** Align member roles with the functional lanes defined in the organizational structure.

Profile the founding/current team:

**For each team member:**
- Name, title, and role
- Background and relevant experience
- Key skills and expertise
- Notable achievements
- LinkedIn/portfolio links
- Time commitment (full-time, part-time, advisor)

**Team Assessment:**
- Collective strengths and gaps
- Complementary skills analysis
- Key hires needed to fill gaps
